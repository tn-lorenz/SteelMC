A map of chunks managing their state, loading, and generation.

pub fn process_unloads(self: &Arc<Self>)

Processes chunks that are pending unload.

This method iterates over the chunks in the unloading_chunks map. If a chunk is only held by the map (strong count is 1), it is removed and a background task is spawned to save it.
